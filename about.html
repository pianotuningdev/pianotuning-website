About

Here is some information about me and my business.

I am a 2023 graduate of the International Piano Technicians School that teaches the Go A.P.E. aural tuning system, which stands for an Accurate, Precise, and Efficent Tuning for your piano.

I began studying piano around the age of 9. I completed all 6 books of the Eda Mae Burnam Piano Course over the course of the next 6 years, which has given me the invaluable skill of reading music.

I received my B.A. in Music from Lewis University in 2008.

I am a member of the Chicago Chapter of the Piano Technicians Guild since November of 2022.

I contine to study piano in the classical and jazz traditions.
