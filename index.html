About Me

Proudly serving the South Side of Chicago!

I began studying piano around the age of 9 (around 1994) under the tutalage of Mrs. Portia M. Harris. I completed all 6 books of the Eda Mae Burnam Piano Course over the course of the next 6 years, which has given me the invaluable skill of reading music.

Under the direction of Joe Caruso, I competed in my early career, in piano competions such as the Illinois Music Association Competition, and Geneva Swedish Days Granquist Piano Competion, where I recevied the Outstanding award for Rachmaninoff's Prelude in C# minor in 2001.

I received my B.A. in Music from Lewis University in 2008.

I am a graduate of the Piano Technicians Academy, and also have received training from respected Registered Piano Technicians from across the world.

I continue to study piano in the classical and jazz traditions.
